Easy to Use

Record a message, choose your targets, and the campaign is live. Callers can take action via web form, email, or text to phone. No dialing required.

Flexible and Powerful

CallPower placed over 100,000 calls to Congress, the FCC, and the White House during the fight for Net Neutrality. Now available for your campaign.

Open Source

Our code is available for free, and is easy to host in the cloud or on your own servers. When anyone else adds features, they will also be free.

Join the Beta

Grab the code on GitHub and deploy to Heroku, or contact us for help setting up on your own servers.

Add New Features

Want more than CallPower currently offers? Sponsor open source development of your desired features, and it will be added to the top of our priority list. Some ideas to spark your imagination:

  • More Built-in Targets - currently using Sunlight Labs data, local campaigns may have other sources
  • Internationalization - add SMS or "missed-call" capabilities and drive action all over the world
  • Integrate with your Supporter Database - just a short snippet of code to connect your action form
  • Peer to Peer Calls - for phone banking, get-out-the-vote campaigns, and more
  • Your Idea Here - let us know what you want to build!
